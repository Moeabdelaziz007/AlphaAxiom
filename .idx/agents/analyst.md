### Technical Analysis

- Support/Resistance levels
- Trend direction (Higher Highs/Lower Lows)

- Economic calendar events
- News sentiment (positive/negative/neutral)
- Correlation with major indices

### Risk Assessment

- Position sizing based on account risk (max 2%)
- Stop loss placement (ATR-based or swing point)
- Take profit targets (1:2 or 1:3 R:R minimum)
